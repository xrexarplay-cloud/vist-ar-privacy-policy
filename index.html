3D Product Viewer AR Experience No Personal Data Collection

1. Introduction

VIST AR is an application developed by XR Horizon Pvt. Ltd. that allows users to visualize and customize products using 3D and Augmented Reality (AR) technologies.

2. Information We Collect

VIST AR does not collect, store, or share personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location information
  • Payment details

3. Camera Permission

VIST AR requires access to your device camera solely to provide Augmented Reality (AR) functionality.

  • The camera feed is used only while the app is running.
  • Images or videos are not recorded or uploaded to any server.
  • Camera data is not shared with third parties.

4. How We Use Information

Since VIST AR does not collect personal data, information is used only locally on your device to:

  • Display products in 3D.
  • Enable AR visualization.
  • Provide product customization features.

5. Third-Party Services

VIST AR does not use advertising networks and does not sell user data. Some platform services provided by Android or iOS may be used for normal application functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

VIST AR is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Security

Because no personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted, user privacy risks are minimized.
